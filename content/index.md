# about me

Hi, I’m Daniel, a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of British Columbia with a strong foundation in mechanical design, composites, and manufacturing. You may know me as the creator of [[WeBWorKeR]], a Chrome extension to enhance UX on the popular WeBWorK homework site. While most of my experience is rooted in mechanical projects, I love exploring and integrating the software side to create problem-focused solutions that make life easier.

My projects often sit at the intersection of mechanics and electronics, driven by curiosity and a desire to improve engineering processes. You can learn more about my projects on my portfolio website, danielrbenjamin.com.
